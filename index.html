Bandon Decker


I am a PhD candidate at the University of Missouri—Kansas City (expected to graduate in May of 2022) studying high-redshift galaxy clusters with Dr Mark Brodwin. I am a part of the Massive and Distant Clusters of WISE Survey (MaDCoWS) collaboration, in which I study the compositions of the high-redshift, infrared-selected MaDCoWS clusters. My main expertise is with optical and near-infrared images and catalogues, but I also have experience with radio data, interferometry, and machine learning algorithms.

On this site, you can find my CV, a more detailed bio and a link to my publications.
