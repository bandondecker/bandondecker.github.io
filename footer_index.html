Bandon Decker, Esq

I am a graduate student at the University of Missouri---Kansas City studying high-redshift galaxy clusters with Dr Mark Brodwin. I have experience with radio interferometry, optical and near-infrared image reduction, catalogue generation, deep data analysis and machine learning algorithms. My expertise is in working with high-redshift OIR sources and I am a skilled programmer and data analyst.
